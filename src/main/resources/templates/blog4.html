Algorithms

4 Feb 2020

Algorithms are the abstraction of directions using particular data structures.

They are independent of any programming language.

While not every language implements them equally, they are most likely capable of being implemented in the majority of them.

The better one understands algorithms, the quicker one will be at discovering solutions to problems. Problems and solutions can be broken into patterns.

The most commonly known algorithms are probably in Euclid and sorting solutions.
